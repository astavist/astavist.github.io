WishLink

A social and interactive wish list platform designed to make gifting more meaningful, efficient, and surprise-friendly.

The Problem

Choosing the right gift is often stressful. Duplicate gifts, unwanted items, wasted time, and the fear of ruining surprises make gifting harder than it should be.

The WishLink Solution

WishLink allows users to create personalized wish lists, connect with friends, and coordinate gifts intelligently without spoiling the surprise.

Personal Wish Lists

Create and share wish lists tailored to your needs and preferences.

Smart Gift Pinning

Prevent duplicate gifts by pinning items privately.

Surprise Protection

Gift coordination happens without the recipient knowing what they’ll receive.
